Text Case Converter

Smart Title Case follows common English rules: don’t capitalize short function words (a, an, the, and, but, or, for, nor, so, yet, as, at, by, in, of, on, per, to, via, vs…), always capitalize first/last words and words after “: ! ? …”, handle hyphenated words, and preserve ALL-CAPS acronyms. Locale affects i/İ (Turkish), Lithuanian dotting, etc.

Tips

  • Only transform selection 可只改选中部分（否则处理整段）。
  • URL slug 会移除重音符号并压缩为 a-z0-9 和连字符。
  • 本工具所有逻辑在浏览器本地执行，离线可用。
